OUR SERVICES

Specialized Restorations offers emergency like services. Our certified technicians will respond to your property to mitigate the water damage.

Category 1

"Clean Water" from a source with no risk of sickness or discomfort. Clean water may possibly be contaminated depending on the environment as it can mix with soil or debris.

Category 2

"Grey Water" has a significant degree of chemical, biological, and or physical contamination. If untreated it can present a significant health risk Our certified technicians will mitigate the damage and remove all affected by the "Grey Water" as it can lead to mold growth.

Category 3

"Black Water" which contains pathogenic agents from sewage or other contaminated water sources. Category 3 or Black water can cause discomfort and or sickness if exposed and untreated.
